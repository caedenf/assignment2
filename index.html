About Mark and his Team

Mark started his painting business back in 2004, painting the homes of his neighbors and others in his community. Now the Mark & Co. Team stands 15-people strong, with specialists ready to assist you in all your painting and home consulting needs.


Mark & Co. proudly offer a 24/7 on-call service, always prepared to take on your own personal painting needs. Our team of consultants will help you create the designs and color schemes you want, and our painters will complete the project in mere days.
